Example: Inline Choice Interaction - Not Working

Question

Identify the missing word in this famous quote from Shakespeare's Richard III.

Now is the winter of our discontent
Made glorious summer by this sun of ;
And all the clouds that lour'd upon our house
In the deep bosom of the ocean buried.

Markup Strategy

This interaction isn't working. The proposed markup strategy is to aria-hide the select box, and build a visually hidden shadow dom that is similar to the other multiple choice, single cardinality, markup presented here in other examples.

However, there is a big problem with this strategy because the <ul> <li> shadow dom elements are block elements. Consequently, they are illegal markup inside inline elements such as paragraph tags.

This example attempts to work around this by using span tags to replace the ul and li tags. AT seems to be very dependent of the list structure, and this span tag replacement strategy does not yield a good result.
